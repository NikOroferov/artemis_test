Creating Purposeful Momentum for your organization

At Artemis, we believe that having a strong purpose for your organization and a strong momentum to drive the purpose are two keys to creating positive social value. Our advisory services will allow you to capably align your corporate CSR work with your core business value using these two principles so that together we can affect the intended impact of your team’s work, and then create measurable & transparent results, which are easily translatable to your stakeholders.

How does it work?

  • 01

    Comprehensive Diagnostic

    Our team performs a top-down diagnostic to precisely evaluate your CSR activities with your business values. The assessment takes into account your purpose & what is the momentum with which it’s being driven, and then define exactly where your team stands on your path to measurable social impact.

  • 02

    Professional Executione

    Our team outlines and defines your specific project or projects that your team can undertake to create sustainable impact & in the process grow your purpose with a progressive momentum. These goals will be outcome-based and focus on measurable results.

  • 03

    Continuous Bottom-Up Tracking

    Our Artemis Impact measurement app backed by our proprietary technology allows your team to engage in continuous, data-driven, bottom-up tracking that provides your team, and your stakeholders, with clear, precise, and transparent data.
