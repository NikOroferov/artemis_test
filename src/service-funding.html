Outcome-based Results Equal Positive Funding

At Artemis, we believe that clearly defined results create significant and measurable social, cultural, and economic impacts. For our individual or institutional donors, & NGOs, our Artemis technology can provide the focused precision in locating, connecting and enabling the most specific targets for your contributions, along with the most transparent and measurable, data-driven results for your stakeholders.

Our services are available

Our services are available and will benefit your corporate teams as well as NGOs and even private enterprises. As always at Artemis, our commitment to impacting significant, effective and measurable social and economic results is our priority. Our experienced consultation service fees will always directly reflect our commitment and we will always work with your team to achieve the measurable success that effects positive change.

How It Works

  • 01

    Pre-funded Projects

    In this scenario, your teams come to us with their own specific project, along with the specific funding/budget this project will have. Our Artemis team then engages our outcome-based consultation services to help your team outline, define, measure, articulate, and activate your goals based on your project specifics and our results-oriented experience and expertise. And, of course, any/all projects will use our proprietary technology to measure their outcome/impact.

  • 02

    Unfunded Projects

    In this scenario, your teams come to us with no specific, or a non-specific project, but with specific funding/budget for their goal. Our Artemis team then engages our outcome-based consultation services to help your donor/receiver team locate, connect and engage with the specific project that best suits your donor/receiver goals, based on our results-oriented experience and expertise. This is the space in which we can most directly serve our corporate and NGO donors with the results-oriented data they require to satisfy their stakeholders. And, of course, any/all projects will use our proprietary technology to measure their outcome/impact.

  • 03

    Funded & Running Projects

    In this case, your team comes to us with projects which are already funded & running, but without clear measurement, and monitoring & evaluating framework. Our Artemis team, then engages our outcome-based consultation services to help you define, execute & report measurement framework. And help your projects be more impact & data-driven. And, of course, any/all projects will use our proprietary technology to measure their outcome/impact.
